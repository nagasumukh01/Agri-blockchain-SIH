📍 Location Access Help

Chrome/Edge:
  1. Look for the location icon (🔒) in the address bar
  2. Click it and select "Allow"
  3. Refresh the page
  4. Click "Detect Location" again
Firefox:
  1. Click the shield icon in the address bar
  2. Select "Allow" for Location
  3. Refresh the page
  4. Click "Detect Location" again
Mobile Devices:
  1. Check if location services are enabled in your device settings
  2. Allow location access for your browser in device settings
  3. Refresh the page
  4. Click "Detect Location" again

Note: If you prefer not to share your location, you can always enter your city name manually.
